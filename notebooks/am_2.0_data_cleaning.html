შეგახსენებთ განმარტებებს:

\[mean = \frac{1}{n} \Sigma_{1}^{n} (x_i)\] \[var = \frac{1}{n} \Sigma_{1}^{n} (x_i - mean)^2 \]

\[sd = \sqrt{var}\]

\[cov(x, y) = \frac{1}{n} \Sigma_{1}^{n} (x_i - mean_x)(y_i - mean_y)\]

\[cor = \frac{cov(x, y)}{sd_x \space sd_y}\]
